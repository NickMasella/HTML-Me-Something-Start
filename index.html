How to code in 5 simple steps

Step 1: Cry

An important first step to any mildly difficult task is to cry about it, and coding is no exception. If you can manage to muster feelings of self doubt and worthlessness this can also be incredibly helpful! Once you've got this step out of the way we can move on to step 2.

Step 2: Begin To Code

®

Figure it out for yourself NERD! If you found a way to read this, good job. You win nothing. Move on to step 3

Step 3: Google

If you've reached this step, you're no where close to being finished with your problem. Congratulations!

Google is incredible and it has all the answers, All hail google! Unfortunately you probably won't be able to understand your search results. So just run your code again for the 60th time and hope something changes. If this fails, which it will, move on to step 4.

Step 4: Cry Again

At this point you're probably thinking about just throwing in the towel and really putting in the effort it takes to solve your problem, But DON'T DO IT! Just cry some more for now. Step 5 is just around the corner.

Step 5: Steal Someone Else's Code

Delete your spaghetti and copy/paste something from the internet. If you're reading this no doubt someone else has already solved your problem.
